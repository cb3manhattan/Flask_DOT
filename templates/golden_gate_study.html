Golden Gate Bridge Bicycle Safety Study

Key insights into bicycle-involved collisions on the Golden Gate Bridge

About the Project

I conducted a bicycle safety study for the Golden Gate Bridge, Highway and Transportation District. I identified key insights regarding existing conditions, usage, and safety on the bridge sidewalks. The graphic presented on this page was made using Excel and Illustrator and shows the number of bicycle-involved collisions occurring along each of the bridges sidewalks.
